02. BASIS OF VALUATION OF FLAT:

Determination of the basis of flat valuation is very troublesome as price of a specific flat in an area is not content. It can very while situating at some area and having some type of convenience, it can strain different price from different buyer depending on many related factors.

a) Whether the land has planning permission,
b) Location and size of flat,
c) Accessibility and availability of resources such as Gas & Electricity,
d) Commercial and residential Importance of the locality,
e) Past trends and future appreciation,
f) Reduce level etc.,

BUILDING:
